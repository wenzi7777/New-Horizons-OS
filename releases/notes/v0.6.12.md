# v0.6.12

## Bug Fixes

### Power Animation on OLED
- Fixed soft power on/off animations not displaying on OLED when configured in `auto` mode. `DisplayManager::startPowerAnimation()` incorrectly checked `config_.mode != "enabled"`, which blocked `auto` mode from starting animations even when the display was detected and enabled. The mode guard has been removed; `enabled_` alone is sufficient to determine display availability.
