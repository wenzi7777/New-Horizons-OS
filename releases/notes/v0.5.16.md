- `python firmware/scripts/generate_arduino_manifest.py --firmware releases/artifacts/newhorizons-os-v0.5.16.bin --output releases/arduino-v0.5.16.json --version v0.5.16 --base-url https://raw.githubusercontent.com/wenzi7777/New-Horizons-OS/v0.5.16/releases/artifacts`
