# New Horizons OS v0.6.8

## Changes

- Fixed FPS still falling below target after the v0.6.7 `yield()` fix. Root cause: `scanAndStreamIfDue()` was called after `imu.service()`, `findme.service()`, `control.service()`, and other services in the main loop. Each time the scan became due, the loop had to complete all preceding service calls (notably the BMI270 I2C reads in `imu.service()`, ~600–1000 µs every 10 ms) before reaching the scan check, delaying the scan start and propagating the miss to every subsequent frame via `scheduleNextScan`'s anchoring to `scanStartUs`. Fixed by moving `scanAndStreamIfDue()` and `sendQueuedPacketIfAny()` to the top of the services block (immediately after `power.service()` and `servicePowerState()`), so the scan fires within ~50 µs of its due time regardless of IMU or display I²C activity.
