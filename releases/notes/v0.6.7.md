# New Horizons OS v0.6.7

## Changes

- Fixed FPS falling below target. The Arduino `loop()` previously called `delay(1)` at the end of every iteration, which invoked `vTaskDelay(1)` and forced a mandatory 1 ms sleep per cycle. Combined with WiFi/service overhead, this quantized scan timing to multiples of ~2–4 ms and resulted in ~41 fps at a 60 fps target and ~78 fps at a 90 fps target. Replaced with `yield()` (`vTaskDelay(0)`), which cooperatively yields to other FreeRTOS tasks without a forced sleep, restoring near-accurate scan timing.
